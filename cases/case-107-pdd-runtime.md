# Case 107: PDD Runtime - Embodied AI Infrastructure

## Company Overview
- **Name**: PDD Runtime (Parallel Distributed Dynamics Runtime)
- **Location**: San Jose, CA
- **Founded**: 2025
- **Stage**: Series A ($12M)

## Focus
Real-time simulation infrastructure for embodied AI training and testing.

## Product
Cloud-based physics simulation platform optimized for robot learning:
- Parallel scene rendering (1000+ simultaneous environments)
- Hardware-in-the-loop testing integration
- Real-time data streaming to robot fleets

## Key Metrics
- **Users**: 50+ research labs
- **Daily Simulations**: 10M+
- **Latency**: <50ms per scene

## competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Isaac Sim | NVIDIA ecosystem | Cloud limited |
| Unity ROS | Easy entry | Slow at scale |
| PyBullet | Free | Limited support |

## Analysis
PDD positions itself as the "AWS for robotics simulation" - providing infrastructure-as-a-service for any robot learning pipeline. The key differentiator is their distributed architecture that can scale from singlerobot testing to fleet-level validation.
